Concordances with Grove’s “Anonymous Theoretical Writings’’

This page shows concordances between the catalog of “Anonymous theoretical writings” by Matthew Balensuela in Grove Music Online and the texts in the TML.

This concordance list has been compiled by the TML team in collaboration with Matthew Balensuela, with the aim to match the TML texts corresponding in whole or part to the treatises identified by Grove’s standard denominations. Note that not every title considered in Grove shows a correspondence with the TML. The list below reflects the catalog published in the New Grove Dictionary of Music and Musicians. Second Edition in 2001.
